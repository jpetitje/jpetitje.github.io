Houston vs Guadalajara cost of living

Houston is a major US city with relatively moderate costs compared to New York or Los Angeles. Guadalajara, often called the "Silicon Valley of Mexico", offers a lower cost of living in many categories.

Quick overview

Housing

Houston rent varies by suburb. Guadalajara rent is typically lower, especially outside premium neighborhoods.

Food

Eating local in Guadalajara is generally cheaper. Imported US products increase costs.

Transportation

Houston often requires a car. Guadalajara offers more affordable public transport options.

Healthcare

Private healthcare costs are typically lower in Mexico compared to the US.
