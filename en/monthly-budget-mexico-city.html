Monthly budget to live in Mexico City

How much do you really need per month to live in Mexico City? The answer depends on lifestyle, neighborhood, and whether you prefer a local or expat setup.

Estimated monthly budget overview

Local lifestyle

Modest rent outside premium zones, local groceries, public transport.

$1,200 – $1,800 USD / month

Comfortable expat lifestyle

Roma, Condesa or similar areas, mix of local and imported goods, frequent dining out.

$2,000 – $3,000 USD / month

Premium lifestyle

Polanco or high-end zones, private healthcare, regular travel.

$3,500+ USD / month

Cost breakdown

  • Rent: biggest variable, highly neighborhood-dependent.
  • Utilities: electricity, water, internet.
  • Groceries: local markets vs imported supermarkets.
  • Transportation: metro, bus, ride-hailing.
  • Healthcare: insurance + occasional visits.
  • Leisure: restaurants, gym, entertainment.

How to calculate your own number

Start with your current monthly budget and adjust using a cost index comparison.

Important considerations

  • Neighborhood choice drastically changes rent.
  • Imported food increases grocery spending.
  • Private healthcare and insurance add stability but increase cost.
  • Frequent flights to the US add a hidden annual expense.
