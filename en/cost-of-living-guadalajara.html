Cost of living in Guadalajara

Guadalajara is one of Mexico’s largest cities and a growing tech hub. It offers a lower cost of living compared to many US cities, while still providing modern infrastructure and amenities.

Estimated monthly budget (USD)

Basic local lifestyle

$1,200 – $1,700 USD / month

Local groceries, modest rent, public transportation.

Comfortable expat lifestyle

$1,800 – $2,800 USD / month

Better neighborhood, mix of local and imported goods, dining out.

Premium lifestyle

$3,000+ USD / month

High-end housing, private healthcare, frequent travel.

Main expense categories

  • Rent: varies greatly by neighborhood.
  • Utilities: electricity, water, internet.
  • Groceries: local markets are affordable; imported goods increase costs.
  • Transportation: buses, ride-hailing, or private vehicle.
  • Healthcare: public vs private options.

How Guadalajara compares to US cities

Compared to cities like Houston, Austin, or Los Angeles, Guadalajara typically offers lower rent and service costs. However, imported goods and premium areas can reduce the difference.
